Some Different Types of Best Protein Powders in India

There are various different types of protein powder but the most common types of protein powder are:

  • Whey: Whey protein is a water-soluble milk protein and is a complete protein which means it has all the amino acids that the human body requires.
  • Casein: Casein protein powder is rich in glutamine and can speed up the recovery after exercise. It is made of dairy and is unsuitable for lactose intolerant and vegans.
  • Pea: Most of the plant-based protein supplement has pea protein which is the best quality alternative to dairy-based and soy proteins.
  • Soy: Soy protein is a perfect alternative to casein or whey protein for people who are lactose intolerant. Plus, it contains all essential amino acids.
  • Hemp:This protein powder supplement is a complete protein that has essential fatty acids and is a perfect choice for vegans or those with soy or dairy allergies.

How Can You Choose Your Best Protein Powder Supplement?

Are you planning to buy the best protein for muscle gain in india? Consider the following things when buying the top protein powder:

  • Read the important information on the back of the protein powder label such as ingredient list and other information. Go through the entire nutrition facts carefully like the amount of protein, number of servings per container and the order of ingredients.
  • Make sure the protein powder you bi has a short ingredient list. This is the best way to avoid proteins with fillers.
  • Opt for a protein powder that provides at least 20 grams of protein per serving and less than 5 grams carbs. To make your protein shake more nutritious you can add milk, banana or any fruit.
  • If you are someone who enjoys different flavours you can opt for flavoured protein powders that come in different delicious flavours like chocolate mint, coffee, cookies and cream, chocolate malt, delicious strawberry, double rich chocolate, french vanilla cream, mocha cappuccino, rocky road, strawberry banana and much more. You can also go for the unflavoured protein powder so that every time you can add the flavour of your choice like cocoa powder vanilla extract etc.
  • Don't buy protein powders that contain artificial sweeteners or added sugar.
  • When buying protein powder look for two parts leucine to one part isoleucine to one part valine. This ratio is perfect for fat loss muscle building and fatigue. If you don't find the amino acids on a protein powder then go with another protein powder.

Ans: Yes, powdered high quality protein powder or protein supplement is good for you as it contains an advanced amino acid profile and helps in increasing protein synthesis.
