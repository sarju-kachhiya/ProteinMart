Some products claim to be good for you and your health while others fall short. Caffeine, sugar, and artificial sweeteners are found in most of the whey protein supplements these days, which means for better results you need to look for the one with less ingredients and no additives or fillers.

Buying guide for cheapest whey protein in India

While making a purchase, you might search for things like ‘cheap Indian whey protein’, and several others but the most important factors leading to a final buy include:

  • Types
  1. Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC): Consists of some fat and carbs along with a lower level of protein (usually 70–80%). It keeps more of the healthy elements that are present in whey.
  2. Whey Protein Isolate (WPI): Has less fat and lactose and greater protein content (sometimes 90% or more)—a great option for people having lactose intolerance.
  3. Whey Protein Hydrolysate (WPH) is a protein that has been partially digested to facilitate digestion. It is frequently included in protein supplements for health.
  • Protein content

Verify if the product has the amount of protein you want. Find out how many grammes of protein each serving a product has, and make sure it fits your nutritional and exercise objectives.

  • Brand reputation

Choose trustworthy brands that are transparent and of high quality. To confirm the product's quality, look for independent testing or certifications.
